About Calmlyte

What is Calmlyte?
Calmlyte is a green-light wellness lighting brand. Its products create a soft, forest-inspired green-light environment in the spaces you live and work in, with four built-in settings for different times of day.
Is Calmlyte a medical device?
No. Calmlyte is a general wellness product, not a medical device. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. Always follow the user manual and discontinue use if discomfort occurs.
What makes Calmlyte different from other lights?
Calmlyte is built specifically around a narrow-band green spectrum and a forest-inspired aesthetic, with settings designed to change the feel of a room through the day rather than simply lighting it.

The light & the settings

What light spectrum does Calmlyte use?
Calmlyte is built around a narrow-band 520–530 nm green spectrum, with 490–500 nm cyan-green and 590 nm amber support depending on the product and the setting.
What are the four settings?
Haven is a balanced everyday setting; Focus is a brighter daytime setting; Stillness is a soft, diffused setting for quiet rooms; and Dusk is a warm, low-intensity evening setting.
How long is a typical session?
Sessions typically range from about 10 to 45 minutes depending on the setting. Start with lower brightness and shorter sessions, then adjust based on comfort and preference.
Can I control brightness and settings?

Do I need to wear the included glasses?
Use the included protective glasses when using a product at close range or higher brightness, and do not stare directly into the LEDs. Individuals with photosensitivity, eye conditions, light-triggered migraines, epilepsy, or those taking photosensitizing medications should consult a qualified healthcare professional before use.

Which product should I start with?

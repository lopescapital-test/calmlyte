## 2. Information we collect

**Information you give us directly**

- Name, shipping address, email, and phone number you enter at checkout to place and receive an order.
- Order details and purchase history.
- Any message you send us (e.g., a support or return request) and our reply.

The site has no account creation, newsletter signup, or contact form.

**Information collected automatically**

- We do **not** use analytics or advertising cookies, and the site sets **no first-party cookies** of its own.
- Our site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which does not provide us with server or access logs, so we do not collect IP addresses or access-log data.
- When you pay, Stripe's checkout page (on Stripe's own domain) may set its own cookies; those are governed by Stripe, not us.

**Information from third parties**

- Payment and transaction confirmation from Stripe. We receive confirmation that a payment succeeded and limited details (such as the last four digits and billing name/postal code); **we never receive or store full card numbers.**

## 3. How we use your information

We use personal information to:

- process and fulfill your order;
- communicate with you about your order (confirmations, shipping, support, returns);
- detect and prevent fraud and secure the site;
- comply with legal, tax, and accounting obligations.

**Marketing:** We do **not** send marketing email or texts. (If Calmlyte later builds a consented list, this requires a consent mechanism, a working unsubscribe, and compliance with CAN-SPAM and — for texts — TCPA before any message is sent.)

## 4. Payment processing

We use **Stripe** to process payments, and Stripe is our only payment processor. In the current flow, adding an item redirects you to a Stripe-hosted Payment Link, so card details are entered on Stripe's page, not ours, and **Calmlyte never receives or stores full card numbers.** Stripe's handling of your payment information is governed by Stripe's own privacy policy: https://stripe.com/privacy.

## 5. How we share information

We do **not** sell your personal information, and we do not "share" it for cross-context behavioral advertising as those terms are defined under California law. (This remains true because the site runs no analytics or ad pixel; if one is ever added, this statement must be re-assessed.)

We disclose personal information only to:

- **Service providers** who help us operate, under contract and only as needed: our payment processor (Stripe) and our shipping carriers (UPS and FedEx);
- **Professional advisers** (accountant, legal counsel) where necessary;
- **Legal authorities**, when required by law or to protect our rights; and
- **A successor entity** in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

We do not share your personal information with any other party.

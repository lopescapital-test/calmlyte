## 1. Return window (change of mind)

You may return most products within **30 days** of delivery for a refund. This change-of-mind window is separate from, and in addition to, your rights if a product is defective (Section 8).

## 2. Condition requirements

- Returned products must be in **unused, like-new condition with all original packaging, accessories, and materials.**
- **Hygiene items.** Because they contact the skin and eyes, an **opened/used Mask cannot be returned for change of mind for hygiene reasons** and is final sale once unsealed, unless defective. The same rule applies to any other skin/eye-contact item once used — the Belt if worn against skin, and (for consistency) the Handheld as a surface-contact device. An unopened item may be returned within the window.
- **Restocking fee:** **None.**

## 3. Non-returnable items

- Opened/used Mask and any other unsealed skin/eye-contact item (see Section 2), unless defective.
- No other items are final sale.

## 4. How to start a return

1. Email **[TBD: monitored inbox]** with your order number to request a return authorization (RMA).
2. We will reply with return instructions and the return address.
3. Pack the item securely in its original packaging and ship it to the address we provide within **14 days** of receiving your RMA.

`[CONFIRM the return logistics: the mailing address on file is PO Box 15191, Pittsburgh, PA. A PO box generally cannot receive large parcels (e.g., the Panel Pro or full-size panel), so you will likely need a street/receiving address for returns of large items. Also confirm whether you issue a prepaid label for change-of-mind returns or the customer arranges shipping — this connects to Section 5.]`

## 5. Return shipping costs

- **Change of mind:** the **customer pays** return shipping. (For a $2,000 Panel Pro this is a material cost the customer should expect; stated deliberately.)
- **Defective, incorrect, or damaged item:** **Calmlyte pays** return shipping and sends a prepaid label. See Section 8.

## 6. Refunds

- Refunds are issued to your **original payment method via Stripe** after we receive and inspect the returned item.
- We process refunds within **5 business days** of completing inspection (your bank may take additional days to post it).
- **Shipping charges:** US outbound shipping is free, so change-of-mind refunds cover the product price. For defective/incorrect/damaged items, we refund the full price and any shipping paid.

## 7. Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. If you want a different product, return the original under this policy and place a new order.

## 8. Defective or incorrect products

If a product arrives defective, or we shipped the wrong item, contact us at [TBD: inbox] within **30 days** of delivery (this does not limit your longer warranty rights). We will, at no cost to you, send a prepaid return label and provide a **replacement or a full refund** including any shipping paid. This is separate from, and more generous than, the change-of-mind path, and is consistent with the Terms of Sale warranty (§7), the limitation-of-liability carve-outs (§8), and the Shipping Policy's damaged-shipment clause (§5).

## 9. Warranty claims

Products carry a **1-year limited warranty** against defects in materials and workmanship (Terms of Sale §7). For a defect discovered after the 30-day return window but within the warranty period, contact [TBD: inbox]; we will repair, replace, or refund at our option.

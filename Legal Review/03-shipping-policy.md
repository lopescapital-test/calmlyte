## 1. Where we ship

We ship to all **50 US states.**

## 2. Processing and delivery time

Products are **made to order.** Your order will be delivered within **10–14 business days** of purchase. This is a total order-to-delivery window (production plus transit), not a separate handling estimate.

## 3. Shipping methods and costs

- **Shipping cost:** **Free** on all US orders.
- **Carriers:** UPS and FedEx.
- **All items ship by air**, including the larger products (Panel Pro and the full-size panel); there is no freight/LTL handling.

## 4. Order tracking

We provide tracking through Stripe. You will receive tracking information for your order after it ships.

## 5. Damaged or lost shipments

This is separate from change-of-mind returns.

- **Damaged on arrival:** Contact us at [TBD: inbox] within **7 days** of delivery with photos of the item and the packaging, and keep all packaging. We will arrange a replacement or refund at no cost to you.
- **Lost in transit:** If tracking shows your order was not delivered, contact us within **30 days** of the expected delivery date and we will investigate with the carrier and send a replacement or refund at no cost to you.

## 6. Address accuracy

Customers are responsible for providing an accurate, complete shipping address. If a package is returned to us because of an incorrect or incomplete address you provided, we will reship it once you cover the reshipment cost; if the error was ours, we cover it.

Overview

The Calmlyte Signature Panel is the signature product in the Calmlyte range: a green-light system built around a narrow-band 520–530 nm green spectrum, with optional 490–500 nm cyan-green and 590 nm amber support for different environments. It offers Bluetooth app control and adjustable brightness, and it anchors all four Calmlyte settings — Haven, Focus, Stillness, and Dusk.

Place it on a desk, nightstand, shelf, or side table to create a softer green-light environment in the room around you. Use it during work breaks, evening routines, reading, meditation, or quiet time when you want the space to feel less harsh and more grounded. Calmlyte is designed as ambient wellness lighting, not as a medical treatment or therapeutic device.

Use with any Calmlyte setting

The Signature Panel is designed to move through the day with you. Each Calmlyte setting shifts the panel's character for a different kind of space:

  • Haven — a balanced mid-morning canopy for everyday calm.
  • Focus — a brighter, crisper daytime reset.
  • Stillness — a soft, diffused setting for quiet, low-stimulation rooms.
  • Dusk — a warm, low-intensity evening environment.

Questions

What is the light spectrum?
The Signature Panel is built around a narrow-band 520–530 nm green spectrum, with optional 490–500 nm cyan-green and 590 nm amber support depending on the setting. Brightness is adjustable, and it connects by Bluetooth app.
Where is it meant to be used?
The Signature Panel is designed for home, workspace, meditation areas, wellness rooms, reading spaces, and quiet evening environments. It is intended to change the feel of a room through soft green-forward ambient light.
How long is a typical session?
Typical sessions range from 10–45 minutes depending on the setting. Focus is designed for shorter daytime resets, Haven for everyday ambient use, Stillness for longer decompression moments, and Dusk for low-light evening transition. Always begin with lower brightness and adjust based on comfort.
Is this a medical device?
No. Calmlyte is a general wellness product, not a medical device. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. Always follow the user manual and discontinue use if discomfort occurs.

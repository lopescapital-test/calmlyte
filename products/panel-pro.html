Calmlyte Panel Pro

The Calmlyte Panel Pro is the full-size Calmlyte light: a green-forward LED panel built around a narrow-band 520–530 nm green spectrum, with optional cyan-green and amber support for different environments. Designed for wellness rooms, studios, and larger spaces, it brings the feeling of soft forest-inspired light to a whole room.

Overview

Position the Panel Pro in a wellness room, studio, treatment space, or larger room where you want to shape the whole environment with soft green-forward light. It offers Bluetooth app control and adjustable brightness across all four Calmlyte settings. Calmlyte is designed as ambient wellness lighting, not as a medical treatment or therapeutic device.

Questions

What is the light spectrum?
The Panel Pro is built around a narrow-band 520–530 nm green spectrum, with 490–500 nm cyan-green and 590 nm amber support. Brightness is adjustable, and it connects by Bluetooth app.
Where is it meant to be used?
The Panel Pro is designed for wellness rooms, studios, treatment spaces, meditation areas, and larger rooms. It is intended to shape the feel of a whole room through soft green-forward ambient light.
Is this a medical device?
No. Calmlyte is a general wellness product, not a medical device. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. Always follow the user manual and discontinue use if discomfort occurs.
